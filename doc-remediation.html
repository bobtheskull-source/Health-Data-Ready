Priority Guidelines

Critical

Immediate legal exposure or active security incident. Requires action within 7 days.

  • Active breach or data leak
  • Missing required privacy policy
  • Processing health data without consent

High

Significant compliance gap or high-risk finding. Action within 30 days.

  • Missing consumer rights process
  • Critical vulnerability (CVSS 9.0+)
  • No data processing agreements

Medium

Moderate gap or general hardening. Action within 90 days.

  • Partial consent mechanism
  • Missing security documentation
  • High vulnerability (CVSS 7.0-8.9)

Low

Best practice improvements or informational items. Action when resources allow.

  • Policy wording improvements
  • Additional monitoring
  • Staff training refresh
